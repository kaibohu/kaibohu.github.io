I am a Senior Research Fellow and Associate Professor, and <a href='https://royalsociety.org/grants/university-research/'>Royal Society University Research Fellow</a> at the [Mathematical Institute](https://www.maths.ox.ac.uk/), University of Oxford.

My research focuses on the intersection of discrete structures, geometry, and computation, aiming to develop rigorous mathematical foundations for structure-preserving numerical methods. By exploring the discrete counterparts of differential geometry, topology, and homological algebra, I seek to build computational frameworks that are inherently compatible with the underlying physical and geometric structures of complex systems.

In this context, I work on **Finite Element Exterior Calculus (FEEC)** and extend these principles into **Finite Element Tensor Calculus (FETC)**, a unifying framework for discrete tensor fields. My work applies these methods to magnetohydrodynamics, continuum mechanics, numerical relativity, and geometric PDEs. Current directions include computational topological (magnetohydro)dynamics, structure-preserving Einstein equations, the development of generalized continua via Hilbert complexes, and finite element approaches to discrete differential geometry and mechanics.

I obtained my PhD at <a href='https://bicmr.pku.edu.cn'>Peking University</a> in 2017, followed by postdoctoral positions at the <a href='https://www.mn.uio.no/math/english/'>University of Oslo</a> (2017–2018) and the <a href='https://cse.umn.edu/math'>University of Minnesota</a> (2018–2020). I joined the <a href='https://www.ox.ac.uk'>University of Oxford</a> (<a href='https://www.maths.ox.ac.uk'>Mathematical Institute</a> & <a href='https://www.chch.ox.ac.uk'>Christ Church</a>) as a Hooke Research Fellow in 2021, later becoming a Royal Society University Research Fellow. Previously, I was a Reader at the <a href='https://www.maths.ed.ac.uk'>School of Mathematics</a>, University of Edinburgh, and <a href='https://www.maxwell.ac.uk/'>Maxwell Institute</a>.

I am the PI of an ERC Starting Grant '<a href='https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101164551'>GeoFEM (Geometric Finite Element Methods)</a>'. The project investigates discretization of geometric objects with finite elements, connections between finite elements and discrete differential geometry and applications.

I am on the editorial board for <a href='https://epubs.siam.org/journal/sjnaam'>SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis</a>.
